About us

Novatech Healthcare, Kolkata, is a pharmaceutical manufacturing company established in the year 2012 in the supervision of Mr. Vikash Modi. We engaged in bringing the society a unique range of pharmaceutical products which are quality tested on different parameters to ensure these are in compliance with the WHO-GMP standards. All our medicines are safe and highly effective. As an integrated healthcare solutions provider, we cater products which includes segments like Analgesic & Anti – Inflammatory, Antiulcer ant, Anti-Biotic, Bronchodilators, Multivitamins and Antioxidants, Ointments and Liquids. Moreover, we strive for optimal products that meet customer expectations.


Novatech Healthcare are working as a team work. All our Suppliers are upholding great role for deliver high-quality products time to time as per our willingness. We have established cordial relations with our customers through Quality Products with Ethical Marketing Support and Professional Work Culture. Our all dealers are star of our team who shines and inspires us every single day to promote our Quality Products in market. Their good work is commendable and we proud to work as a co-partner.


Novatech Healthcare have so many opportunities for dedicated professionals who are committed for the welfare of mankind and ready to serve the healthcare industry in a proficient manner, since we are looking for enthusiastic and self-starter with an experience and aspiration to change for a bold new career opportunity.


Why Choose us

We have Quality Products with Ethical Marketing Support and Professional Work Culture for enthusiastic and dedicated professionals.
